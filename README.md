# pSI-snRNA
To systematically characterize the transcriptional landscape of the pSI and uncover its underlying sex differences, we employed 10× single-nucleus RNA sequencing (snRNA-seq). Given that the pSI remains a relatively unexplored region, the sex-biased molecular signatures of its neurons are completely unknown. By applying snRNA-seq to the pSI tissue, we successfully profiled 36,522 cells and identified 22 distinct cell clusters. Crucially, this single-cell resolution allowed us to delineate the intrinsic transcriptional properties of pSI neurons, providing the first unbiased blueprint to decode the sexually dimorphic gene expression networks within this region.
# Data Availability
Single-nucleus gene expression datasets of pSI in male and female have been deposited at GEO and are publicly available as of the date of publication. GEO: GSE326514.
